About the FishQuery Tool


The FishQuery Tool is designed to find a list of fresh water aquarium fish based on selected attributes such as tank size, temperature and other requirements. Currenty there are 457 fish in the database. This contains no saltwater fish or fish bigger than 60cm in length. There are 24 different attributes that you can select from. Give it a try!
